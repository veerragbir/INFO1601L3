Why Won't Lecturers Be More Lively?

Why won't lecturers be more lively? This is something I don't take lightly. Come to class and read some slides, And then you wonder why students hide.

Why won't lecturers be more engaging? It's something that is quite enraging. You read some notes and then I say, "Why do I even come to class today?"

Why won't lecturers be more fun? That's all it takes to be #1. I don't duck class, I want to pass, But my tiktok brain just can't last.

Why won't lecturers be more exciting? Is it that? Or am I just not inviting? Are these just excuses for lax in attention? Maybe I just need some gumption.

Why won't I just do what's right? Come to labs and study at night. No more excuses, no more slacking, Just get to work and stop skylarking.
